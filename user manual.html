User Manual

StockSight Cloud: Personal Portfolio Intelligence

1. Introduction

StockSight Cloud is a sophisticated, modular portfolio intelligence tool designed to help investors analyze stocks structurally. By removing emotional bias through data-driven scoring, it assists in managing portfolio allocation effectively. It leverages real-time data from robust sources including Screener.in, Yahoo Finance, and AMFI.

2. Getting Started

Accessing the App

Open welcome.html in your browser. Click the Enter Terminal button to load the main dashboard.

The Interface

  • Sidebar (Left): Your command center. Use the search bar here to add stocks or view your watchlist items.
  • Main Dashboard (Right): The primary workspace displaying Portfolio cards, Watchlist cards, and deep Analytics.
  • Tabs (Top): Seamlessly switch between "My Portfolio", "Watchlist", and "Analytics" views.
  • Stocky Button (Bottom Right): Your gateway to the AI assistant.

3. Managing Assets

Adding Stocks/ETFs/MFs

  1. Navigate to the Sidebar.
  2. Type the ticker symbol (e.g., TCS, HDFCBANK) or the Mutual Fund Scheme Code (e.g., 120503).
  3. Press Enter or click the Add button.
  4. The system automatically fetches data. If a stock isn't found immediately, an advanced auto-search triggers to locate the correct symbol.

Building Your Portfolio

Once a card appears in the Watchlist tab:

  • Locate the input fields for Qty and Avg price on the card.
  • Enter a quantity greater than 0.
  • The card automatically migrates to the My Portfolio tab.
  • Real-time P&L is calculated instantly based on the live price versus your entered average.

Removing Assets

  • From Watchlist: Hover over the item in the sidebar and click the red "DEL" button.
  • From Portfolio: Simply set the Qty to 0. The card moves back to the Watchlist, where it can be deleted.

5. Stocky AI Assistant

Stocky is your intelligent chatbot. Click the floating robot button to interact.

Capabilities

  • Analyze Assets: Ask "Explain ITC" or "Is Tata Motors a buy?". Stocky will fetch data even if it's not in your list.
  • Compare Stocks: Ask "Compare HDFC vs ICICI".
  • Risk Check: Ask "Is my portfolio risky?".
  • Portfolio Allocation: Ask "Invest 1 Lakh for me". Stocky generates a weighted allocation strategy based on asset quality scores.

Advanced Commands

  • "What is the target for [Stock]?": Reveals Moreshwar dynamic levels.
  • "Why is the score low?": Drills down into the specific metric dragging the score down.

6. Analytics Tab

The Analytics tab offers a high-level portfolio X-ray:

  • Asset Allocation: Visual breakdown of Equity vs Cash vs Gold/ETFs.
  • Performance: Total Portfolio P&L tracking.
  • Health Score: Weighted average quality score of your holdings.
  • Action Plan: Automated, actionable suggestions (Buy/Sell/Review) based on live data.

7. Troubleshooting

"Asset Not Found"

Ensure you use the correct NSE/BSE symbol. Try typing the full name (e.g., "Reliance Industries") instead of just "RIL". For Mutual Funds, use the AMFI Code.

"Score is 5 / Data Insufficient"

Common for Finance/Bank stocks where "Operating Profit Margin" (OPM) is N/A. The system normalizes this, but occasional data source variations may trigger a safety penalty rather than a zero-out.

"Data Not Loading"

The app relies on public proxies. If the network is slow, wait a moment or click the refresh button in the sidebar.
